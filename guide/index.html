What is Hoosh? ‚Äã

Hoosh is a command-line AI assistant built in Rust that brings the power of multiple AI backends into your development environment. It's designed for developers who want intelligent assistance without leaving their terminal.

Key Highlights ‚Äã

  • Multi-Backend: Support for OpenAI, Anthropic, Together AI, and Ollama
  • Secure: Permission-based system with review and autopilot modes
  • Fast: Built in Rust for maximum performance
  • Flexible: Configure everything to match your workflow
  • Context-Aware: Maintains conversation history intelligently
